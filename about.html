About NGKFlip

A cleaner way to check Amazon deal leads.

NGKFlip is built for shoppers who want quick access to interesting Amazon finds without digging through clutter.

What we share

We highlight products, Amazon categories, and deal leads that may be useful for bargain hunting, personal shopping, or flip research. Prices can move quickly, so the final check always happens on Amazon.

Why we use affiliate links

Some links on NGKFlip are Amazon affiliate links. If you purchase through one of those links, NGKFlip may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Transparency matters

NGKFlip participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.
